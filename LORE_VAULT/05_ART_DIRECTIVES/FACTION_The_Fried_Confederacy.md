# HQ VISUAL BIBLE: The Fried Confederacy
*Status: PRODUCTION-READY | Vector-Lock Enabled*

## 0. THE CORE IDENTITY
**The Fried Confederacy** is a deeply splintered, high-heat alliance. Built on the grueling labor of dedicated fryer lines, they operate in the sweltering, oil-slicked pockets of Lumenridge. They are less of a corporate monolith (like the Grill Houses) and more of a tense, heavily armed coalition of distinct cultural sub-factions.

---

## 1. THE CREW ROSTER & COLOR LOCKS
*To be enforced strictly in all AI prompting and vector color-flattening.*

### The Colonials (Archetype: KFC)
* **The Vibe:** Crisp, militaristic, patriarchal. They claim to be the founding fathers of the Confederacy, strictly adhering to the "Original Recipe" of governance.
* **Vector Color Lock:** **Bone White & Crimson**
* **Uniforms:** Heavily starched white shirts, black string ties, grease-stained aprons. 

### The Southern Insurgency (Archetype: Popeyes)
* **The Vibe:** A deeper, hotter, more chaotic kitchen culture. They actively challenge the Colonials' dominance, utilizing Cajun-inspired brutality and faster, more aggressive line movements.
* **Vector Color Lock:** **Rust Orange & Brick Red**
* **Uniforms:** Utilitarian, sweat-soaked, orange visors, reinforced heat-resistant gloves (burn scars are a mark of rank).

### The Fundamentalists (Archetype: Raising Cane's)
* **The Vibe:** A zealous sect with absolute doctrinal purity. They produce exactly one product. They view menu diversification as a weakness. A cult of high-efficiency youth laborers.
* **Vector Color Lock:** **Oxblood & Pale Gold**
* **Uniforms:** Casual but uniform to a terrifying degree. Matching polo shirts, headset mics, dead-eyed efficiency.

### The Diaspora Network (Archetype: Jollibee)
* **The Vibe:** Fiercely guarded community loyalty. They operate outside the standard hierarchy, untouchable by the larger empires because their supply lines and loyalty are locked tightly within diaspora neighborhoods.
* **Vector Color Lock:** **Scarlet & Marigold**
* **Uniforms:** Clean lines, chef hats, bright jackets, but hardened with concealed carry and an unblinking, cheerful ruthlessness.

---

## 2. INTERNAL POLITICS (The Civil Wars)

* **The Blood Feud:** The Colonials vs. The Southern Insurgency. The Colonials own the massive distribution hubs, but the Insurgency controls the streets. It's a constant, simmering proxy war over fryer territory.
* **The Isolationists:** The Fundamentalists refuse to work with anyone who "contaminates the fryer." They sit out the faction wars, hoarding resources in their hyper-efficient, highly-defensible strongholds.
* **The Untouchables:** The Colonials have tried to absorb The Diaspora Network for a decade. The Network simply smiles, declines, and protects its borders with terrifying, community-backed efficiency.

---

## 3. EXTERNAL DIPLOMACY (The Street Matrix)

* **Active Alliance — The Slice Syndicates (Pizza):** Both factions operate in high-heat environments and rely heavily on route logistics in the poorer sectors of Lumenridge. They regularly trade excess fryer oil for safe passage on delivery routes.
* **Kill-on-Sight — The Grill Houses (Burgers):** The eternal war. The Grill Houses view the Confederacy as disorganized, greasy savages; the Confederacy views the Grill Houses as sterile, soulless corporate fascists. 
* **Tense Neutrality — Ghost Oil Refineries:** The Confederacy uses more raw cooking oil than any other faction in the city. They are completely dependent on Ghost Oil, giving the supplier massive political leverage over them—which the Confederacy resents deeply.

## 4. VISUAL & ARCHITECTURAL ANCHORS
*For use in all concept art and panel generation.*

* **Lighting:** Heavy, oppressive, and warm. Amber heat lamps, the violent glow of boiling oil, harsh fluorescent flicker bleeding out from the walk-in freezers. 
* **Atmosphere:** The air should look thick. Plumes of steam, suspended grease, sweat on skin.
* **Architecture:** Blocky, defensible structures. Strip-mall concrete reinforced with corrugated steel. Drive-thrus converted into armed chokepoints and toll booths. 
* **Vector Note:** When vectorizing these environments, emphasize the stark, sharp shadows cast by the heat lamps. The grease on the floor should be rendered as hard-edged, reflective puddles, giving the final image a slick, dangerous comic-book finish.
