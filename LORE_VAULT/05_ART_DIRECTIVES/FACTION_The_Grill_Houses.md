# HQ VISUAL BIBLE: The Grill Houses
*Status: PRODUCTION-READY | Vector-Lock Enabled*

## 0. THE CORE IDENTITY
**The Grill Houses** are the dominant corporate monolith of Lumenridge. They represent mass production, political lobbying, and brute-force volume. They operate out of highly visible, high-traffic intersection corner lots. Their power comes from absolute standardization and an endless supply of disposable teenage labor driven by sociopathic drive-thru timers.

---

## 1. THE CREW ROSTER & COLOR LOCKS
*To be enforced strictly in all AI prompting and vector color-flattening.*

### The Arches Crew (Archetype: McDonald's)
* **The Vibe:** The ruling class. Corporate, clean on the outside, ruthless on the inside. They set the laws of the fast-food tier. 
* **Vector Color Lock:** **Oxblood & Tarnished Gold**
* **Uniforms:** Crisp, synthetic polo shirts. Earpieces for drive-thru coordination that double as tactical comms. Plastic smiles.

### The Crown Syndicate (Archetype: Burger King)
* **The Vibe:** The eternal rival. Looser corporate discipline, grittier outposts. They constantly try to usurp the Arches through aggressive territory expansion and flame-broiled intimidation.
* **Vector Color Lock:** **Midnight Blue & Burnt Ochre**
* **Uniforms:** Paper crowns worn unironically as gang markers. Greasy, flame-scorched aprons. 

### The Frostbite Crew (Archetype: Wendy's)
* **The Vibe:** The scrappy, cynical challenger. They refuse to cut corners (square patties as a political statement). They are known for brutal PR and a fiercely loyal, slightly unhinged workforce.
* **Vector Color Lock:** **Crimson & Frost Blue**
* **Uniforms:** Retro-fitted 80s pinstripes, heavy frost-blue winter jackets (walk-in freezer gear worn as armor).

---

## 2. INTERNAL POLITICS (The Civil Wars)

* **The Throne War:** The Arches and The Crown Syndicate have been in a cold war for decades. The Arches control the legislation; The Crown controls the suburban sprawl. Skirmishes at shared intersections are common.
* **The Square Doctrine:** The Frostbite Crew considers both the Arches and the Crown to be compromised by frozen logistics. They act as a rogue state, occasionally teaming up with the Crown just to hurt the Arches.

---

## 3. EXTERNAL DIPLOMACY (The Street Matrix)

* **Active Alliance — Ghost Oil Refineries:** The Grill Houses are the largest buyers in the city. Their alliance with Ghost Oil is the bedrock of Lumenridge's economy, though it is purely transactional.
* **Kill-on-Sight — The Fried Confederacy:** The Confederacy hates the Grill Houses' sterile corporate structure. The Grill Houses view the Confederacy as chaotic insurgents disrupting the market.
* **Tense Neutrality — The Fresh Syndicate:** The Grill Houses watch the rising "eat fresh" propaganda with suspicion, knowing it threatens their dominance in the lunch-rush sector.

---

## 4. VISUAL & ARCHITECTURAL ANCHORS
*For use in all concept art and panel generation.*

* **Lighting:** Blinding, sterile fluorescent overheads. The neon glow of menu boards. Flashing red LEDs from the drive-thru timers dictating the pace of life.
* **Atmosphere:** Artificial and pressurized. The smell of aerosol sanitizer mixed with heavy grease. Everything surfaces are smooth plastic or stainless steel.
* **Architecture:** Corner lot fortresses. Drive-thrus are designed like bank teller security tubes. Giant, oxidized golden arches acting as territorial monoliths.
* **Vector Note:** Emphasize the stark, clinical lighting. The contrast between the bright, plastic customer areas and the dark, stainless-steel-and-grease reality of the kitchen should be pushed to the extreme. No soft gradients.
