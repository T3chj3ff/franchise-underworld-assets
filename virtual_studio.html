The Arches Crew

Tier 1 Elite
Vibe & Stereotype Corporate, ruthless, highly organized mafia. They project a sterile, perfect image to hide a cutthroat reality.
Art Direction: Lighting & Background Stark, clinical overhead fluorescents. Environments are perfectly sterile stainless steel kitchens contrasting heavily with bright, synthetic plastic seating areas (FOH vs BOH divide). Zero grime in the front, pure grease in the back.
Visual Locks Oxblood & Tarnished Gold
Coin: 3 Overlapping Arches
Lingo / Motifs
"The Order" "McDoom" "Happy Meal" (an assassination)

The Crown Syndicate

Tier 2 Royals
Vibe & Stereotype Gritty, flame-scorched, aggressively expanding. Dethroned royalty looking to reclaim the top spot through brute force.
Art Direction: Lighting & Background Dim, smoky interiors constantly filled with thick flame-broiler smoke. Blighted commercial zones, cracked asphalt, and flickering drive-thru menus. Heavy shadows.
Visual Locks Midnight Blue & Burnt Ochre
Coin: A Jagged Crown
Lingo / Motifs
"Have it your way" (a threat) "The King's Tax" Paper Crowns

The Frostbite Crew

Tier 2 Rogues
Vibe & Stereotype Scrappy, cynical, defensively armored. "Never-frozen" purists who operate like a highly defensive rogue state.
Art Direction: Lighting & Background Cold blue walk-in freezer lighting. Frost on the windows. Derelict freezer trucks used as mobile bases and heavily armored, fortified drive-thru windows.
Visual Locks Crimson & Frost Blue
Coin: Crystallized Snowflake
Lingo / Motifs
"Square Up" "Four Corners" "Fresh Blood"

The Grand Slammers

Tier 3 Neutral
Vibe & Stereotype Exhausted, loyal, completely numb to urban chaos. They run the 3 AM shift where rival bosses and hitmen eat in peace.
Art Direction: Lighting & Background Yellow-tinted, buzzing, warm diner lighting. Formica tables, fake leather booths, heavy rain on plate-glass windows at 3 AM. A sanctuary vibe.
Visual Locks Mustard Yellow & Rust
Coin: Bold Blocky '4'
Lingo / Motifs
"The 3 AM Shift" "Slammed" Heavy Under-Eye Bags

The Grid Iron

Tier 3 Brawlers
Vibe & Stereotype Frontline combatants, violent, hyper-alert. They don't run from fights, they start them behind the counter.
Art Direction: Lighting & Background Harsh, flickering bare bulbs. Sticky floors, shattered glass, yellow caution tape aesthetics, and exposed brick. Action-oriented framing.
Visual Locks Hazard Yellow & Asphalt Black
Coin: Waffle Grid
Lingo / Motifs
"Scattered, Smothered, Covered" (A body) "On the Grid" Taped Fists

The House of Plates

Tier 2 Suburbia
Vibe & Stereotype Medicated, unsettlingly synchronized, fake family-friendly. Hides an industrial batter-pumping operation behind a smile.
Art Direction: Lighting & Background Overly bright, almost painfully sunny daylight or sickly sweet pastel interiors. Hides dark, massive, industrial steel batter vats in the deep background.
Visual Locks Indigo & Crimson
Coin: Towering Stack of Plates
Lingo / Motifs
"Syrup Trap" "Short Stack" (A quick kill) Forced Smiles

The Bloomin' Cartel

Tier 1 Monopoly
Vibe & Stereotype Faux-rugged luxury, strict militaristic kitchen brigades. The wealthy cartel that controls the premium meat supply.
Art Direction: Lighting & Background Dim amber pendant lighting, polished mahogany booths, taxidermy mounts, and heavy, warm shadows. Looks expensive but dangerous.
Visual Locks Mahogany & Hunter Green
Coin: Concentric Onion Rings
Lingo / Motifs
"Bushwhacked" "The Outback" Excessive Flair

The Roadhouse Riders

Tier 2 Supply
Vibe & Stereotype Rowdy, loud, heavy denim and boots. They control the supply routes and enforce loyalty with mandatory rituals.
Art Direction: Lighting & Background Air choked with peanut dust. Neon beer signs cutting through the haze, rough-hewn wood, and sawdust-covered floors.
Visual Locks Barn Red & Saddle Brown
Coin: Branded Crossway 'T'
Lingo / Motifs
"Line Dance" (A coordinated hit) "Riding the Route" Brass Knuckle Buckles

The Fallen Stars

Tier 4 Mercs
Vibe & Stereotype Washed up, faded glory, black-market operators. They lost the steakhouse war and now survive on the fringes.
Art Direction: Lighting & Background Skeletal remains of opulent 90s steakhouses. Broken neon, ripped leather booths, heavy dark shadows hiding illicit deals.
Visual Locks Faded Denim & Tarnished Silver
Coin: Single 5-Pointed Star
Lingo / Motifs
"Faded" "Tarnished" "Star-crossed"
